Mike's Story

My career passion is to build and develop creative things. Individually and professionally, I am a front-end web developer, motion graphics producer, traditional graphic designer, video editor, audio engineer, stereoscopic 2D to 3D conversion specialist, creative and art director, editor, copywriter, photographer and musician.

Collectively, I lead and inspire creative teams that build brands up via smart and artistically creative collateral development. I've been instrumental in helping to make the companies I've worked for hundred's of millions of dollars by building in-house creative divisions that did not previously exist and turning them into award-winning, multi-million dollar making creative juggernauts.

The first 17 years of my career were spent working for AMC Theaters advertising affiliate National Cinema Network in Kansas City, Missouri, leaving as Vice President of Creative Services, Creative Director to rise to Senior Vice President of Media and Production Services at National CineMedia, LLC, in Denver, Colorado, departing in 2017 after 12 years there.
